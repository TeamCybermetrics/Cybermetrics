# Team Contract

---

## 1. Purpose
This contract outlines how we will work together throughout the course. Its goal is to ensure clear expectations, consistent communication, and shared responsibility for all team tasks and deliverables.

---

## 2. Team Expectations

### a) Preparation
- Members will complete assigned readings and individual tasks before meetings or tutorials.  
- Anyone arriving unprepared accepts consequences agreed on by the team (e.g., reduced peer evaluation score, small team penalty like buying snacks).

### b) Communication
- Primary communication platform: **Discord, Instagram**.  
- Members will reply within **8–12 hours** on weekdays unless otherwise stated.  
- All communication must remain respectful, constructive, and professional.

### c) Attendance & Engagement
- Members will attend all scheduled meetings and tutorials unless illness or emergencies occur.  
- If a member cannot attend, they must notify the team ahead of time and share their input asynchronously.  
- Everyone is expected to contribute ideas and stay actively involved during discussions.

### d) Decision-Making
- We aim for consensus whenever possible.  
- If consensus cannot be reached, we will vote; **majority wins**.  
- Major project changes require team approval; minor adjustments may be made individually.

### e) Work Quality
- Work should be completed on time, tested, and meet the team’s quality standards.  
- Members will review each other’s work and provide constructive feedback.  
- Every major Pull Request should be reviewed by at least one teammate.

---

## 3. Conflict Resolution
- Conflicts will first be discussed respectfully within the team.  
- If the issue cannot be resolved internally, a TA will be asked to help mediate.  
- In severe or unresolved cases, the instructor may be involved.

---

## 4. Accountability
- All members are responsible for completing their share of the work and asking for help early if needed.  
- Repeated lack of participation may affect peer evaluations.  
- Members will provide honest and fair peer evaluations at the end of milestones.

---

## 5. Agreement
By signing below, each member confirms they understand and agree to this contract. Any revisions must be approved by the entire team.
